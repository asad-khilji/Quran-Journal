Surah Al Fatihah: Ayah 2

ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

The extremely loving and caring right now, the always extremely loving and caring.

What dua can you make from this ayah?

O Allah! bestow upon me Your unimaginable and incredible love and care.

What advice is Allah personally giving you in this ayah?

Allah is advising me to call upon His two most beautiful names, Ar Rahman and Ar Raheem.

What question does this ayah raise for you?

What is the difference between these two names?

With the name Ar Rahman, Allah is taking care of my immediate needs, and with the name Ar Raheem, Allah is taking care of my future.

How does this ayah have an affect on you for a moment in your life?

Allah takes care of me constantly out of His unconditional love and care.
