Surah Al Fatihah: Ayah 6

صِرَٰطَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ

The path of those whom You have favored.

What dua can you make from this ayah?

O Allah guide me upon the path whom You have favored, those who had beneficial knowledge and acted upon it, the path of the Prophets, Truthful, Martyrs, and the Righteous. Whose path you made easy.

What advice is Allah personally giving you in this ayah?

Allah is advising me to look at the lives of the Prophets, those who confirmed truth in the Prophets, the martyrs, and the righteous and follow their example in order to be upon the straight path

What question does this ayah raise for you?

Who are the people on the straight path?

The Prophets, those who confirmed truth in the Prophets, the martyrs and the righteous.

How does this ayah have an affect on you for a moment in your life?

I look at stories of the Prophets, companions, those who are martyred, and the righteous and find advice relevant for me to strive on the straight path.
