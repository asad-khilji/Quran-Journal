Surah Al Fatihah: Ayah 7

غَيْرِ ٱلْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا ٱلضَّآلِّينَ

But not the path of those who had wrath upon them nor of the lost?

What dua can you make from this ayah?

O Allah do not make us from those who had wrath upon them nor of the lost.

What advice is Allah personally giving you in this ayah?

Allah is advising me to seek beneficial knowledge and act upon it to stay on the straight path.

What question does this ayah raise for you?

Who are these people who incur wrath and the lost?

Those who had beneficial knowledge but did not act upon it and those who had no knowledge and acted in a wrong manner.

How does this ayah have an affect on you for a moment in your life?

I continue to learn beneficial knowledge and always try to act upon that which I have attained in knowledge.
