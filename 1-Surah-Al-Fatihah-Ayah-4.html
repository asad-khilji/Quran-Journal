Surah Al Fatihah: Ayah 4

إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ

You alone do we we willingly enslave ourselves to and You alone do we desperately seek help from.

What dua can you make from this ayah?

O Allah grant me the ability to worship You alone and to ask for Your help sincerely after exhausting myself in effort

What advice is Allah personally giving you in this ayah?

Allah is advising me to give my best efforts so He can send His help to me and that is the recipe of success (my efforts + Allah’s help = success)

What question does this ayah raise for you?

What are we asking for help in?

We are asking for Allah's help in everything and we are so desperate and in need of help that we are short on words and we just say help.

How does this ayah have an affect on you for a moment in your life?

Allah has helped me in my life in countless ways.
