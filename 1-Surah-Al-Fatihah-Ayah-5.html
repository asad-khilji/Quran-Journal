Surah Al Fatihah: Ayah 5

ٱهْدِنَا ٱلصِّرَٰطَ ٱلْمُسْتَقِيمَ

Guide us upon the straight path that is upwards.

What dua can you make from this ayah?

O Allah guide me upon the straight path.

What advice is Allah personally giving you in this ayah?

Allah is advising me to constantly ask for His guidance several times a day every time we stand in salah.

What question does this ayah raise for you?

Why is the straight path upwards?

Going up requires effort and it is not easy to be on the straight path with so many temptations pulling you down faith goes up and down never stationary you must keep going higher

What is the straight path?

In Surah Al Fatihah Allah mentions the name Ar Rahman and In Surah Rahman Allah mentions the name Ar Rahman. In Surah Al Fatihah Allah says we need to be on the straight path. In Surah Al Rahman Allah says we need a balance and to balance our life. So the straight path is a balance in your life.

How does this ayah have an affect on you for a moment in your life?

Everyday I am faced with decisions to make and to get guidance I turn to Allah’s book and find guidance in it.
