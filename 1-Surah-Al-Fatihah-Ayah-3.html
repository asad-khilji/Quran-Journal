Surah Al Fatihah: Ayah 3

مَـٰلِكِ يَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ

The Owner and the King of the day of judgment.

What dua can you make from this ayah?

O Owner and King grant me direct access to the highest levels of the Jannah without any accountability.

What advice is Allah personally giving you in this ayah?

Allah is advising me to prepare for the Day of Judgment by doing as many good deeds as I can.

What question does this ayah raise for you?

Why does Allah mention these names here?

First Allah mentioned His love and care then He mentioned His justice to balance everything so we do not take advantage of Allah’s love and care for us.

How does this ayah have an affect on you for a moment in your life?

Everytime I look up at the sky I see how the sky is raised high and balanced by Allah. Then I am reminded of the day of judgment when our deeds will be put on the scale to be balanced and judged based on our actions.
